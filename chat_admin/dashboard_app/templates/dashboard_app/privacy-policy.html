COOKIES AND SIMILAR TECHNOLOGIES

We receive different types of data from the computers and/or servers that you use when you access the Site. “Cookies” are small files placed on your computer by a Web site, in order to facilitate the use of that website. Pixel tags are tiny graphic files that can function in various ways (such as allowing us to track how you view an email that we send you) and are commonly used in conjunction with cookies. Local storage is similar to a cookie but is instead located on your browser and can store more information. When using this site and services, we may create “cookies” on your computer for the purpose of facilitating and maintaining your current use session, and may collect personal information using those cookies in order to enable Afrometa to identify you in connection with the Services and to track your use of the Services. Other data that we receive when you use our Sites includes “clickstream information” such as your IP address, the type of operating system and/or browser that you use, your location, and the pages that you visit. You are free to decline our Cookies if your browser or browser add-on permits, unless our Cookies are required to help prevent fraud or help ensure the security of our Services. You can configure your Internet browser, to accept all cookies, reject all cookies or notify you before accepting a cookie. Many Internet browsers also allow you to disable local storage or delete information contained in local storage. Each Internet browser is different, so check the "Help" menu of your browser to learn how to change your cookie preferences. For information on disabling or deleting information contained in local storage, please click here. However, declining our cookies may interfere with the use of the Services.
